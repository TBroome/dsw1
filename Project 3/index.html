What it takes...

Every Morning:

Think of a thousand pounds, sitting on your back, at the same time you have a suction cup pulling every ounce of energy out of your body. That is the feeling you have every single morning when you deal with depression. There is always that weight sitting on you telling you that you are worthless would be better off dead. When you face that fight every day your only thought is why today? Why should I get up today? Well MY reason is my daughter and my pets. As silly as it sounds that little fluffy white thing sleeping on my back and that crazy 3 year old are what drive me out of bed everyday and that fill my heart with something other then pain.

You don't have to control your thoughts. You just have to stop letting them control you. - Dan Millman.

Symptoms Of Depression:

  1. Persistent sad, anxious, or "empty" mood
  2. Loss of interest or pleasure in activities, including sex
  3. Restlessness, irritability, or excessive crying
  4. Feelings of guilt, worthlessness, helplessness, hopelessness, pessimism
  5. Sleeping too much or too little, early-morning awakening
  6. Appetite and/or weight loss or overeating and weight gain
  7. Decreased energy, fatigue, feeling "slowed down"
  8. Thoughts of death or suicide, or suicide attempts
  9. Difficulty concentrating, remembering, or making decisions
  10. Persistent physical symptoms that do not respond to treatment, such as headaches, digestive disorders, and chronic pain

Ways you can help!

Symptom (ref to numbers above) Way To Help
1-3 Take them outside for a while, go for walks with them
4 Remind them of ways they have helped others
5 Help them have a set time to go to bed and to wake up, dont let them sleep in to much or sleep to little
6 (7 by association) Make them eat!!! They wont want to MAKE THEM This is noe of my biggest problems and trust me YOU NEED TO EAT!!
8 Remind them of all the reason they have to stay. DONT tell them it will hurt others that will only make them feel worse
9 Help them make to-do lists and actually complete them
10 Understand that these are not just in the persons mind and make allowances to make their life a little easier.

Pushing Through

Even with all this hell I am living with i push through as i know there are people who are depending on me. BUT not everyone can deal with the pain and heartache that depression can bring so please reach out and help someone you know who deals with depression. Even if they say they don't need it trust me they do.
